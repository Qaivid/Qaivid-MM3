Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your account settings at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free.

What counts as a project?

One project is one music video run — from lyrics upload through storyboard, stills, and final video assembly.

What is the commercial licence?

Pro and Studio users can release, sell, and monetise the videos generated by Qaivid without restriction.

Do you offer annual billing?

Monthly billing only at launch. Annual plans with a discount are coming soon — sign up to be notified.

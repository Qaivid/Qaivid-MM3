How credits work

🪙 1 credit = 1¢

Every monthly plan charges exactly 1 cent per credit. Yearly plans give you the same credits at a lower per-credit cost.

🎬 Animatic Video

100 credits = 1 minute of animatic (Ken Burns animated storyboard). Ideal for previewing your production before full AI rendering.

🎞️ AI Cinematic Video

500 credits = 1 minute of full AI-generated cinematic video. Higher compute cost, photorealistic output.

🖼️ Still Images

10 credits per AI still image generation. Face-locked stills via PuLID cost the same as standard generations.

Credits reset monthly. Unused credits do not roll over. Small credit deductions also apply for AI steps (transcription, briefs, images).

Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your account settings at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free.

Do credits roll over?

No — credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused credits from the previous month are not carried forward.

What is the commercial licence?

Starter, Pro, and Studio users can release, sell, and monetise the videos generated by Qaivid without restriction.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes — switch to Yearly on the toggle above to save 10–20% depending on your plan. Annual plans are billed upfront.

What counts as 1 minute of video?

60 seconds of rendered output timeline — whether animatic or full AI video. Shot duration accumulates across all scenes in your project.

Can I buy extra credits?

Top-up credit packs are coming soon. For now, upgrade to a higher plan for more monthly credits.

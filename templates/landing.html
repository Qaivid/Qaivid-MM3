How it works

From first word to finished film
— end to end

MetaMind 3.1 handles every stage of production. Just bring your audio or script — no editing skills, no crew required.

🎤
Step 1

Input & Analysis

Drop in a track, paste lyrics, or write your script. MetaMind 3.1 auto-transcribes, analyses BPM, detects beats, and maps your content to a precise timeline.

🧠
Step 2

Creative Direction

MetaMind 3.1 runs a full context audit — characters, locations, tone, narrative arc — then generates a complete director's brief and visual style treatment.

🎞️
Step 3

Storyboard

Every line is broken into timed shots with camera angles, motion cues, and mood. Character and location reference plates are generated for visual consistency across the film.

🎬
Step 4

Production

Each shot is rendered as a face-locked cinematic still, then animated into a real AI video clip — character-consistent, beat-synced, and motion-prompted by MetaMind 3.1.

🏆
Step 5

Post-Production & Export

Clips are stitched into a final film with audio mixing, animated subtitles, corner logos, cinematic colour grading, and a fully export-ready master — ready to publish.

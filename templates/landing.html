How it works

From lyrics to cinematic shot list
in four steps

No video editing. No design skills. No guesswork. Just paste, upload, and let the AI direct.

🎤
Step 1

Drop in your content

Paste your lyrics, poem, or script. Upload your audio file in any format. MetaMind handles the rest — even if you only have one of the two.

🧠
Step 2

AI builds your creative brief

MetaMind generates a full director's brief — visual style, tone, narrative arc, characters, and locations — giving every shot a consistent artistic identity.

🎞️
Step 3

AI generates every still

Each storyboard scene is rendered as a face-locked cinematic still — with character consistency, framing, motion cues, and mood all controlled by MetaMind.

🎬
Step 4

Review, refine & export

Step through each scene, edit framing and meaning, approve your character reference — then generate your complete beat-synced video storyboard in one click.

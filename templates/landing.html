How it works

From lyrics to cinematic shot list
in three steps

No video editing. No design skills. No guesswork. Just paste, upload, and let the AI direct.

01
🎤

Drop in your content

Paste your lyrics, poem, or script. Upload your audio file in any format (MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A). Qaivid handles the rest — even if you only have one of the two.

02
🧠

AI builds your storyboard

The AI transcribes your audio in 40+ languages, reads the emotion and story, maps every lyric to its exact beat, and generates a full director-grade shot list — framing, motion cue, mood, and visual prompt for every scene.

03
🎬

Review, refine & generate

Step through each scene, edit framing and meaning, approve your character reference plate — then generate face-locked cinematic stills for every shot in one click.

What makes it different

Speaks every language

Punjabi, Hindi, Arabic, Urdu, French, Spanish, English — the transcription engine handles any script and any language with native-grade accuracy, preserving the original Gurmukhi, Devanagari, or Arabic characters exactly as sung.

Beat-synced shot timing

Every lyric line is mapped to its exact timestamp in the audio — instrumental intros, long sustained notes, and rapid-fire bars are all handled correctly. Scenes start and end exactly where the music does.

Upload a single reference photo of your artist or character. Every generated still uses that face, locked in — no drift between shots. The AI keeps your character visually consistent across the entire video.

8 cinematic shot types, real framing directives (ECU, medium, wide, POV), camera motion cues, body language details, and mood tagging — every shot reads like it came from an actual director's shot list.
